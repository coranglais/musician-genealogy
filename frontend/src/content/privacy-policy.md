# Privacy Policy

**The Musician Genealogy Project**

Last updated: March 2026

## What This Project Is

The Musician Genealogy Project is a non-commercial, community-driven reference resource for documenting pedagogical lineages of musicians — who studied with whom, where, and when. It is not operated for profit and does not sell advertising or user data.

## What Information We Collect

### Publicly Displayed Data

The site displays biographical and pedagogical information about musicians, including names, birth and death dates, nationalities, instruments, institutional affiliations, and teacher-student relationships. This is factual, publicly available reference information comparable to what appears in music dictionaries and encyclopedias.

### Information You Provide When Contributing

If you submit a contribution through our submission form, we collect:

- Your name
- Your email address
- The information you provide about musical lineages and relationships
- Any supporting notes or verification details you include

### Information We Collect Automatically

We collect basic server access logs (IP addresses, timestamps, pages visited) for security and abuse prevention purposes. We do not use third-party analytics, tracking cookies, or advertising networks.

## How We Use Your Information

### Contributor Information

Your email address is used to:

- Verify your submission (via a confirmation link)
- Allow you to check the status of your submission
- Contact you if an editor has questions about your submission

Your email address is **never** displayed publicly on the site, shared with third parties, sold, or used for marketing purposes. Only site editors can see contributor email addresses, and only in the context of reviewing submissions.

Your name, if you consent, may be credited in a contributors list. You may submit anonymously or under a pseudonym if you prefer, though this may affect our ability to verify your submission.

### Publicly Displayed Musician Data

Biographical and pedagogical data about musicians is displayed publicly. This information consists of factual reference data (names, dates, institutional affiliations, teacher-student relationships) drawn from published sources, community contributions, and publicly available records.

If you are a musician listed in our database and wish to have your information corrected or removed, please contact us.

## Data Storage and Security

Submission data is stored in a secured database hosted by Railway (a cloud hosting provider). Access to contributor email addresses and submission metadata is restricted to site editors.

We use reasonable security measures appropriate for a non-commercial community project, including encrypted connections (HTTPS), secure session handling, and access controls on administrative functions.

## Data Retention

- Contributor email addresses are retained for as long as needed to process and verify submissions. You may request deletion of your email address at any time by contacting us.
- Publicly displayed musician data is retained indefinitely as part of the reference database, consistent with the project's archival purpose.
- Server access logs are retained for a limited period for security purposes.

## Your Rights

You have the right to:

- **Access** any personal information we hold about you
- **Correct** inaccurate information about you in our database
- **Request deletion** of your email address and contributor information
- **Request removal** of your biographical entry if you are a listed musician

To exercise any of these rights, contact us at the email address below.

## Cookies

This site uses only essential cookies required for site functionality (such as admin session cookies). We do not use advertising cookies, tracking cookies, or third-party analytics cookies.

## Children

This project is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe a child has submitted personal information through our contribution form, please contact us.

## Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated date.
